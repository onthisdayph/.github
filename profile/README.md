## The Problem

> Significant moments in Philippine history fade from public memory.\
> History resources are dry and inaccessible.\
> Misinformation spreads faster than facts.

**OnThisDay.PH** solves this by building a permanent, community-verified **archive of significant Philippine events** — covering politics, culture, disasters, milestones, and everything in between. Every entry is paired with shareable content and backed by at least one credible source.

---

## What We Cover

OnThisDay.PH is **not limited to politics**. We record and resurface significant events across all aspects of Philippine life:

- **Politics & Governance** — Elections, legislation, scandals, and policy changes
- **Culture & Society** — Arts, traditions, social movements, and cultural milestones
- **Natural Disasters & Calamities** — Typhoons, earthquakes, and how communities responded
- **Economy & Industry** — Economic shifts, major business events, and labor movements
- **Science & Innovation** — Filipino achievements, discoveries, and breakthroughs
- **Sports & Entertainment** — Historic wins, cultural icons, and national pride moments

Our goal is to make Philippine history accessible, engaging, and impossible to forget — so that citizens can learn from the past and hold the future accountable.

---

## How It Works

| Stage | What happens |
|:---|:---|
| **📝 Submit** | Community members submit an event, meme, and source URL. |
| **🔍 Verify** | Admin reviews and approves valid submissions. |
| **🗳️ Vote** | Community upvotes/downvotes to surface credible entries. |
| **📢 Share** | Content cards are optimized for social posting. |
